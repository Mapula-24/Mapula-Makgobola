Types of candles

Scented candles:These essential oil-infused candles are ideal for creating a warm atmosphere because they emit a range of fragrances, from energising citrus to soothing lavender.

Decorated candles:These candles stand out as ornamental elements that give personality to any space because of their distinctive forms, hues, and patterns.

aromatherapy candles:These candles use medicinal essential oils for a comprehensive experience and are specifically made to inspire energy, attention, or relaxation.
